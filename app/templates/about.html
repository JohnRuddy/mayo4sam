About Mayo4Sam

Personal Project

Mayo4Sam is a personal project created to solve a simple problem: staying up-to-date with Mayo football news scattered across different Irish sports websites. Instead of visiting multiple sites daily, this application brings all the Mayo-related stories together in one place.

As a Mayo football supporter, I found myself constantly checking various sports news websites to catch the latest updates. This became time-consuming and I often missed stories. Mayo4Sam automates this process, saving time and ensuring no important Mayo news gets overlooked.

How It Works (In Simple Terms)

1. Data Collection

The application automatically visits Irish sports news websites every 30 minutes and reads their RSS feeds (a standardized way websites share their latest articles).

2. Filtering

From all the GAA stories collected, it keeps only those that mention "Mayo" in the title or content, filtering out stories about other counties.

3. Organization

The Mayo stories are organized by date with the newest first, and duplicate stories from different sources are handled intelligently.

4. Presentation

Everything is displayed in a clean, mobile-friendly format with search and date filtering options to help you find exactly what you're looking for.
